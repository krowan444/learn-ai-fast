Why I created Learn AI Fast

Technology should make life simpler, not smaller.

I kept seeing people who were curious about AI but felt shut out by technical language, fast-paced videos and advice that assumed too much.

Learn AI Fast is my answer to that. A calm place to ask the “obvious” questions, try things together on your own device, and understand both the opportunities and the risks.

Whether you’re curious, cautious, helping a parent, or wondering where to begin, you belong here.
