For businesses: one cloned voice call can cost you thousands

Criminals now clone a director's voice from three seconds of audio and phone your finance team with an "urgent payment". Staff spot the fake barely a third of the time — and UK firms lost hundreds of millions to exactly this kind of fraud last year. One hour of training gives your team the instincts and the simple verification habits that stop it: call-back rules, code words, and what never to do on a voice request alone. Cheaper than one successful scam, by a very long way.
